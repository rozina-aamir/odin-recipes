Lasagna

Description

A lasagna (also known as a lasagne) is a layered pasta dish that uses wide and flat pasta sheets.

Other contents of a lasagna include meat, white lasagna sauce, and cheese.

This recipe tells you how to create a delicious lasagna from scratch.


Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 750g lean beef mince
  • 90g pack prosciutto
  • 800g passata sauce
  • 200ml hot beef stock
  • nutmeg
